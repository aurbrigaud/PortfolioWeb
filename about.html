(About)

Graphic designer and art director based in Paris, working across visual identity, editorial and digital design. I build concept-driven systems where form follows strategic intent, translating creative strategy into cohesive design frameworks. With seven years of experience, including awarded and international collaborations, my practice balances typographic precision and visual clarity to shape forward-looking narratives for brands and institutions worldwide.

Available for collaborations with brands, institutions and studios.  
For new projects or collaborations, feel free to get in touch.
